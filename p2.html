About eSports

eSports in Saudi Arabia has witnessed remarkable growth in recent years, with the kingdom emerging as a regional and global hub for the industry. The Saudi eSports Federation was established in 2017 to oversee and develop the eSports community and the industry surrounding it in the kingdom.

Objectives of the Saudi eSports Federation

  • Develop the eSports industry in the Kingdom.
  • Enhance participation in local and international tournaments.
  • Support and nurture young talent in the field of eSports.
  • Forge partnerships with global companies to develop eSports.
  • Promote Saudi Arabia as a regional eSports hub.

Achievements of the Saudi eSports Federation

  • Organizing local and international tournaments such as "Gamers Without Borders" and "Season of Gamers".
  • Hosting the eSports Olympics 2025.
  • Qualifying Saudi clubs for global competitions like Al-Nassr and Al-Ittihad clubs.
  • Launching the National eSports Academy.
  • Organizing the "Saudi Cup" for eSports in collaboration with international entities.
  • Hosting major global events like the "FIFA 2024" and "PUBG Mobile World Championship".
  • Supporting emerging talents through training programs and scholarships.
  • Building strategic partnerships with major tech companies like Sony and Microsoft.
