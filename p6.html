Major Achievements and Partnerships

Saudi esports teams have made significant strides in recent years thanks to substantial financial and strategic support from government entities and private companies. Notable sponsors and partnerships include:

SAVVY Games Group

Launched by the Public Investment Fund with investments totaling SAR 142 billion, it included the acquisition of ESL and FACEIT, two of the largest esports tournament organizers globally, merging them into the ESL FACEIT Group to enhance the Kingdom's position in this sector.

Saudi Aramco

Saudi Aramco has become the main strategic partner for the Esports World Cup. The company aims to enhance its role in supporting this sector and nurturing local talent through major partnerships.

Partnerships with Saudi Esports Teams

Falcons

  • Partnership with STC Play to provide exclusive content and develop tournaments and training activities in esports.
  • Collaboration with Manga Productions to support integrated entertainment content including anime and esports.

Power

  • Al Rajhi Bank: Supports the team with "Gamers" cards, and their logo appears on the team kits.
  • Shawarma Restaurants: Partnership to enhance the team's visibility through joint promotional campaigns.
  • Technology companies: Collaborations to develop the academy and organize local tournaments.

Twisted Mind

  • MOONTON Games: A key partner of the team, supporting their participation in major tournaments such as the M6 World Championship for Mobile Legends: Bang Bang.
  • ASUS: The team has partnerships with technology companies specializing in computers and gaming equipment to enhance the players' performance.

The Kingdom's Vision for Esports

These partnerships reflect the Kingdom's vision to transform esports into a key sector within the national economy and achieve a leading global position.
